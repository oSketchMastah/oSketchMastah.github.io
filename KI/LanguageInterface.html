Language Knowledge Interface

This interface is designed to guide you through a comprehensive knowledge-base dedicated to language and communications

There are two major aspects to every language, namely its phonetics, and its semantics. Every word may be represented as a sequence of symbols (each corresponding to a sequence of phonemes) or an equivalent sequence of unique sounds called phonemes. Understanding the phonetics of the underlying alphabet is what makes the translation from symbol to sound possible, whereas understanding the semantics of the sounds/symbols is what allows us to any information communicated to us in a unique way.
